How does FCOVID works

F/COVID is the non-profit arm of Hackers/Founders and HACK Fund, established to deliver technological solutions for the COVID-19 crisis. Our Innovation Challenges are constructed to address healthcare professionals’ urgent needs as they serve on the front lines.


Our Process
-Network with government, healthcare and manufacturing leaders
-Source and select nonprofit startup teams from around the globe
-F/COVID provides on-demand funding for validated hardware and software solutions
-The F/COVID team is available for support

We build. We measure. We improve. We improvise. We overcome.
