Hackers and Founders fighting COVID-19

COVID-19 is a once-in-a-century pandemic likely to infect millions. Our healthcare heroes are working long hours in difficult conditions to save lives. And they need the help of brilliant hackers and entrepreneurs who can bring innovation to an often antiquated system. Following are open challenges. Let’s do our part to fight COVID-19 worldwide.

About FCOVID

F/COVID is the nonprofit arm of Hackers/Founders and HACK Fund, established to deliver technological solutions for the COVID-19 crisis. Our Innovation Challenges are constructed to address healthcare professionals’ urgent needs as they serve on the front lines.
